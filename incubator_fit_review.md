# Incubator Fit Review: PROPHIT

## Verdict
Strong fit

## Why it fits HACD
- Prediction market staking benefits from PoW-backed formation cost — participants have real skin in the game from day one
- HACD containers provide credible, visible cost basis for each prediction token lot
- Community formation around Stack lots mirrors the social competition of prediction markets
- Durable formation history creates transparent provenance for the token supply
- Scarce HACD containers enforce hard supply cap — no inflation dilutes predictor rewards

## What HACD adds
- PoW container: Each PROPHIT lot is tied to a specific HACD, giving the prediction token a verifiable formation origin
- Formation cost: 30 HAC stack cost per HACD ensures participants commit real value before entering the prediction ecosystem
- Asset history: On-chain formation records create transparent, auditable supply
- Community formation: Prediction market participants form the token supply together, building community from genesis

## Main concerns
- Prediction market utility depends on platform development (oracle, resolution mechanism)
- Must clearly state utility is planned, not guaranteed at launch
- Must avoid any language suggesting guaranteed returns from predictions

## Required issuer confirmations

- Confirm whether governance utility is included at launch or planned
- Confirm max HACD per participant
