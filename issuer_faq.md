# FAQ: PROPHIT (PRT)

## Do I need HACD?
Yes. Each Stack participation requires at least 1 HACD unit according to the launch rules.

## Do I need HAC?
Yes. HAC is needed for the 30 HAC stack cost per lot and network fees.

## Is this an investment product?
No. This is a HACD Stack Asset launch for a prediction market utility token. No profit, return, or price appreciation is guaranteed.

## How does the prediction market work?
Participants stake PRT on market predictions (e.g., price direction, event outcomes). Correct predictors earn PRT from the pool of incorrect stakers. Details of the resolution mechanism will be published during platform development.

## When will prediction features be live?
Platform development follows token formation. No specific launch date is guaranteed. The token formation on HACD Launchpad is the first step.

## What is the maximum I can participate with?
Each participant may Stack between 1 and 5 HACD lots, producing 5,000 to 25,000 PRT.

## What happens if I remove the Stack?
The 5,000 PRT tied to that HACD lot are permanently burned. Your HACD is released. The 30 HAC stack cost is not refunded.

## Is there a team allocation?
No. All 200 lots are public and equal. No reserved lots, no team allocation, no designated addresses.

## Can the supply increase?
No. Once all 200 lots are Stacked, total supply is permanently fixed at 1,000,000 PRT. The only direction supply can move is down (through burns via Stack removal).

## Who reviews this before launch?
HACD Labs reviews all Launchpad parameters. This is a draft pending their approval. Issuer must confirm all numbers before publication.
