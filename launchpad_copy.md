## What is PROPHIT?

PROPHIT (PRT) is a prediction market token formed through HACD Stack. It powers an ecosystem where participants stake PRT on market predictions and earn from accuracy.

Every prediction costs something real. Every correct call earns something real. No cheap talk — only PoW-backed conviction.

## Why HACD?

Prediction markets need skin in the game. HACD Stack provides exactly that:

- **Real formation cost**: Each PRT lot costs 30 HAC + HACD to form. No free tokens, no airdrops.
- **Fixed supply**: 1,000,000 PRT total. Once 200 lots are Stacked, no more can ever be created.
- **Transparent provenance**: Every PRT unit traces back to a specific HACD formation event.
- **Burn-on-removal**: Unstacking destroys PRT permanently, reinforcing scarcity.

Bitcoin proved PoW for money. HACD brings PoW to assets. PROPHIT brings PoW to predictions.

## How Stack Works

1. Hold at least 1 HACD unit
2. Prepare 30 HAC per HACD for stack cost (plus network fee)
3. Visit the Launchpad and select PROPHIT (PRT)
4. Enter your HACD name(s) and confirm the Stack transaction
5. Receive 5,000 PRT per HACD lot Stacked

Each HACD lot produces exactly 5,000 PRT. All 200 lots are equal — no tiers, no reserved allocation.
