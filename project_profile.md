# Project Profile: PROPHIT

## One-liner
A PoW-formed prediction market token where participants stake their calls and earn from accuracy — back your conviction on-chain.

## Category
Utility / Community / Prediction Market

## Problem
Crypto market predictions are cheap talk. Anyone can post "BTC to 100k" with zero accountability. There is no cost to being wrong and no verifiable track record for being right. Prediction markets exist, but few tie participation cost to a durable, PoW-backed formation process.

## Asset concept
PROPHIT (PRT) is a fungible Stack Asset formed through HACD. Each PRT unit represents a stake in the PROPHIT prediction ecosystem. Holders use PRT to enter prediction markets, stake on outcomes, and earn from correct calls. Top predictors build on-chain reputation backed by real formation cost.

## Why HACD
- Formation cost = skin in the game: every PRT has a visible, non-trivial creation cost
- PoW-backed scarcity enforces fixed supply — no minting dilution to bail out wrong predictions
- HACD container history proves token provenance and formation date
- Stack logic aligns with prediction market thesis: commit value upfront, earn through accuracy

## Target users
- Crypto traders and analysts who want to prove their alpha publicly
- Community members who enjoy competitive prediction games
- Builders who want a PoW-native utility token with clear purpose
- HACD holders looking for meaningful utility from their containers

## Launch readiness
Draft stage. Core token design complete. Platform utility (prediction interface, oracle, leaderboard) is planned for post-launch development. Token formation and supply structure are ready for Launchpad.
